Physics & Computational Engines

A collection of high-performance N-body, vector mechanics, and orbital simulations built with WebGL and Custom Solvers.

01 / Pendulum Phasemap

Millions of double pendulums calculated to generate an interactive fractal phase map.

02 / Rope & Pendulums

Stable N-link pendulums using RK4 integrators and Position-Based Dynamics for rope physics.

03 / Fluid Dynamics

Real-time Navier-Stokes solvers with high-resolution vortex visualization.

04 / Gravity & Orbits

3D vector mechanics engines calculating momentum, gravity, and chaotic N-body systems.

05 / Electromagnetism

3D particle trajectories in complex magnetic and electric fields using Lorentz force integration.
