Physics & Computational Engines

A collection of N-body and vector mechanics simulations built with Python, SciPy, and WebGL by Sarvas77.

01 / Double Pendulum Fractal Phase Map

Phase map rendering millions of double pendulums at high fps.

02 / Interactive N-Pendulum

A stable, interactive N-link pendulum simulation utilizing the RK4 integrator.

03 / Physical Pendulum & Rope Physics

A stable physical pendulum and true continuous mass rope simulation.

04 / 3D Galaxy Simulation

A 3D N-body orbital engine with a massive central black hole.

05 / Chaotic 3-Body Dance

An elastic collision ring handling a chaotic 3-body system.

06 / Fully 3D 4-Body Stick Orbit

A 3D vector mechanics engine calculating momentum, gravity, and angular torque.
